About Me

Hi, I’m Simon.

I work as a data journalist and newsroom developer at correctiv.org, a journalism non-profit based in Germany.

I learned how to be a journalist at the Cologne School of Journalism and attended some classes in economics and politics at the University of Cologne.

When writing, I’m into economics & politics in other countries, for example in Rwanda. I’m also the founder and former editor-in-chief of an award-winning students magazine. In 2015, I was a fellow of the Vocer Medialab. Plus, I organize a series of meetups related to data driven journalism.

When developing, I’m into django, reactjs and do also some data wrangling with pandas (and yeah, even R, because every data journalist does that, right?) or some visualisations with several other libraries.
